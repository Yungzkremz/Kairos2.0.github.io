About Us

Kairos International School Amawbia is a prestigious institution dedicated to providing quality education to students from various backgrounds. Our school focuses on academic excellence, holistic development, and nurturing the leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision & Mission

At Kairos International School Amawbia, our vision is to inspire and empower every student to achieve academic success and become responsible global citizens. Our mission is to provide a conducive learning environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and personal growth.

Programs Offered

We offer a wide range of programs designed to cater to the educational needs of students at different levels, including:

  • Primary School Program
  • Junior Secondary School
  • Senior Secondary School
  • Advanced Level Programs

Admission Process

Admissions to Kairos International School are open to students who meet our academic requirements and share our values of discipline, responsibility, and academic excellence. To apply, please visit our Admission page form or contact us directly for more information.
